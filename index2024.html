A little bit about myself...
At the age of 16, on my way back home from school, I got a phone call. On the other end was the CEO of Israel’s top leading design firm. I sent him my portfolio a few days earlier, and he suggested we meet. I took a train to the big city, and as we met, he offered me to join his firm. I replied that I should probably finish high school first. A year later, we met again, and my professional journey began.

With the firm being a one-stop-shop, I got to work on a vast array of projects, from designing large banking systems, to corporate websites, Flash games, and many more, combining my love of design, animation and code.

A few years later, after working with clients such as Microsoft, Orange, Nokia, MSD and many more from Israel and abroad, I moved into tech.
After consulting for a number of early stage startups, I joined MyHeritage, one of Israel’s leading startups. As the company grew into a 500 employee organization, my role evolved into my current position as Director of UX, managing a large group of Product Designers, Marketing Designers and UX Researches. Revamping the entire product line, introducing new data-driven design processes, user-centered roadmap planning, scaling up the team, taking the native app to the 1st place on the American AppStore, creating the company’s first ever Design System, have been some of the many initiatives I’ve led in the company.

Oh, and I love to draw and animate stuff, especially quirky architecture from around the globe.
