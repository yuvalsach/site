A little bit about myself...

During my junior year in high school, at the age of 16, on my way back home from school, I got a phone call. On the other end was who was at the time the CEO of Mantis, Israel’s top leading design firm, and the head of the Visual Communication Studies in Israel’s Art and Design Academy. I sent him my portfolio a few days earlier, and he suggested we meet. In response to his offer that I join the firm, I replied that I should probably finish high school first. A year after, my professional journey had began.

With the firm being a one-stop-shop, I got to work on a vast array of projects, from designing large banking systems, to corporate websites, interactive Flash games, and many more, combining my love of design, animation, and code. A few years later, after working with clients such as Microsoft, Orange, Nokia, MSD, and many more from Israel and abroad, I moved into tech, first as an external consultant for a number of startups, and later on as an in-house Product Designer, as I joined MyHeritage, one of Israel’s leading startups. Starting as the second designer on the team, my role took many shapes and forms as the company grew into a 500 employee company. Revamping the entire product line, introducing new data-driven design processes, user-centered roadmap planning, scaling up the team, taking the native app to the 1st place on the American AppStore, creating the company’s first ever Design System, have been some of the many initiatives I’ve led in the company.

I now act as the Director of UX & Design at MyHeritage, managing a large group of Product Designers, Marketing Designers and UX Researches.

Oh, and I love to draw and animate stuff, especially quirky architecture from around the globe.
